## Critical Success Tips

1. **Always call retention department** — not general support
2. **Have competitor quotes ready** before you call (real numbers = real leverage)
3. **Get everything in writing** — email confirmation of any rate changes
4. **Be ready to actually switch** — empty threats don't work
5. **Call Tuesday–Thursday** — avoid Mondays and end-of-month (lower authority reps)
6. **Ask "what else can you offer"** — always a second layer of discounts
